The Future of the Celtics

HOME



The future of the Boston Celtics is very bright. Having one of the 3 yougnest teams in the NBA along with a bevy of future draft picks gives the future celtics a lot of promise. Current players on the team on their way to stardom include Jayson Tatum (19), Jaylen Brown (21), Marcus Smart (23), Terry Rozier (23) and a top 25 player already Kyrie Irving (25). The Celtics currently have 6 first round draft picks over the next 3 years, which is unheard of for a team performing at this level.
